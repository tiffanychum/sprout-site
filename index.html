Support

Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you need? Email us — we reply within 24 hours.

Is Sprout free to use? +
Yes. Sprout is free forever for 1 plant — including Daily Bloom, the Plant Placement Compass, care reminders, and 1 AI diagnosis per month. Sprout Pro unlocks unlimited plants and diagnoses.
How does the AI diagnosis work? +
Take two photos: one full-plant shot and one leaf close-up. Sprout's AI analyses them for symptoms — yellowing, brown edges, drooping — and produces a numbered treatment plan in order of urgency. The two-photo approach gives significantly more accurate results.
What is the Plant Placement Compass? +
It's a guide to finding the best spot for each plant in your home. Tap any of the 8 compass directions to discover which plants bring a sense of calm, focus, or growth to that area. Each plant shows its recommended placement and the qualities it supports — helping you create a space that feels intentional and alive.
How do care reminders work? +
Sprout builds a personalised watering and fertiliser schedule based on your plant species and pot size. If you miss a day, a gentle push notification keeps your plant on track. Mark as watered directly from the notification.
Can I use Sprout offline? +
Care reminders, the memory journal, and the plant catalogue all work offline. AI diagnosis requires an internet connection to process photos.
How do I cancel Sprout Pro? +
Open the App Store → your profile → Subscriptions → Sprout → Cancel. Your data is never deleted and the cancellation takes effect at the end of the billing period.
