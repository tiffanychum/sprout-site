Support

Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Email us and we'll reply within 24 hours.

Is Sprout free to use? +
Yes. Sprout is free forever for 1 plant, including Daily Bloom, the Feng Shui compass, care reminders, and 1 AI diagnosis per month. Sprout Pro unlocks unlimited plants and unlimited diagnoses.
How does the AI diagnosis work? +
Take a photo of your plant (ideally one wide shot and one leaf close-up). Sprout's AI analyses the images for symptoms — yellowing, brown edges, spots, drooping — and produces a prioritised treatment plan. The more photos you provide, the more accurate the diagnosis.
What is the Plant Placement Compass? +
It's a Feng Shui Bagua guide built into the app. Tap any of the 8 compass directions — North, South, East, West, and the four diagonals — to see which plants invite luck, harmony, abundance, or peace in that area of your home. Each plant shows its element, fortune meaning, and placement tips.
How do care reminders work? +
Sprout builds a personalised watering and fertiliser schedule based on your plant species and pot size. If you miss a day, a gentle push notification reminds you. You can mark plants as watered directly from the notification or the Today screen.
Can I use Sprout offline? +
Care reminders, your memory journal, and the plant catalogue all work offline. AI diagnosis requires an internet connection to process your photos.
How do I cancel my Sprout Pro subscription? +
Open the App Store → tap your profile → Subscriptions → Sprout → Cancel. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period. Your data is never deleted.
